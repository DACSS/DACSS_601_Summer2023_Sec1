DACSS 601: Data Science Fundamentals

Summer 2023

The blog posts here are contributed by students enrolled in DACSS 601, Fundamentals of Data Science. The course provides students with an introduction to R and the tidyverse, scientific publishing, and collaboration through GitHub, building a foundation for future coursework. Students also are introduced to general data management and data wrangling skills, with an emphasis on best practice workflows and tidy data management.
